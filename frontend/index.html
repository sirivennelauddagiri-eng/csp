The Urban Waste Challenge

In rapidly growing cities, sanitation departments often struggle to keep up with scattered waste issues. Traditional reporting is slow, opaque, and disconnected from the community it serves.

  • Delayed response times to critical sanitation issues.
  • Lack of transparency in issue resolution for citizens.
  • Inefficient resource allocation due to missing data.

The Smart Solution

PublicSpace bridges citizens and city authorities in real time, using AI-powered verification and geospatial intelligence to transform community feedback into fast, effective action.

Smart Geotagging

Pinpoint exact locations automatically with GPS integration.

AI Verification

Instantly validate reports through image recognition technology.

How It Works

1

Report

Snap a photo of the unclean area, add a quick description, and submit. Your location is pinned automatically.

2

Verify

Our system and community moderators verify the report, ensuring resources are directed to real issues.

3

Resolve

Sanitation crews are dispatched. You get a notification once the space is cleaned and rated 'Clear'.
